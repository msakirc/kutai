## 1. What it is

Two distinct things share the "Mage" name and conflate easily:

1. **Mage.ai (mage-ai/mage-ai on GitHub, mage.ai)** — an open-source data-pipeline / orchestration platform; AI-native data engineering. NOT an app builder. Frequently mis-listed in "best AI app builders 2026" articles as a confusing inclusion.
2. **MAGE (research project, "GPT-powered open-source platform for full-stack web app generation using Wasp/React/Node/Prisma")** — academic-style OSS predecessor to v0/Lovable circa 2023-2024. Original repo activity has slowed; not a 2026-active commercial product.
3. **trymage.com** — no substantial 2026 footprint surfaced. Either rebranded, abandoned, or never reached escape velocity.

## Lesson for KutAI

The "Mage" brand is a cautionary tale about naming collisions in the AI-tools space. A name that's been claimed by multiple tools across categories (data, app gen, image gen) actively harms discoverability. KutAI's Turkish-character internal naming (Yaşar Usta, Fatih Hoca, etc.) is unique enough to avoid this trap, but the user-facing "Kutay" is generic Turkish and may collide with future tools.
