# Brand Service Network Map -- Turkey

## Arçelik / Beko / Altus / Grundig
- **Parent**: Koç Holding (Arçelik A.Ş.)
- **Service Network**: 600+ yetkili servis, 4,500+ hizmet noktası across Turkey
- **Coverage**: Excellent nationwide, including rural areas
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard (white goods), 3 years compressor (fridges), 10 years motor (washing machines on select models)
- **Spare Parts**: Readily available, 10+ year parts guarantee for major appliances
- **Service Speed**: Typically 1-3 business days in cities, 3-7 days in rural areas
- **Call Center**: 444 0 888 (Arçelik), 444 0 232 (Beko)
- **Notes**: Best service infrastructure in Turkey. Altus and Grundig (TR) use same service network. Local manufacturing means fast parts supply. Online service appointment via arcelik.com.tr.

## Samsung
- **Service Network**: 400+ yetkili servis noktası
- **Coverage**: Strong in major cities, good in Anatolia
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard, 10 years Digital Inverter motor (washing machines), 20 years Digital Inverter compressor (select fridges)
- **Spare Parts**: Good availability for recent models, imported parts can take 1-2 weeks
- **Service Speed**: 1-3 days in major cities, up to 7 days elsewhere
- **Call Center**: 444 77 11
- **Notes**: Local assembly in Turkey (Tuzla) for some products. Mobile phone service via Samsung Experience Stores (Istanbul, Ankara, İzmir). Galaxy phone screen repair often same-day at experience stores.

## LG
- **Service Network**: 250+ yetkili servis noktası
- **Coverage**: Adequate in cities, weaker in rural areas
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard, 10 years Smart Inverter motor, 10 years Linear Compressor
- **Spare Parts**: Imported -- can take 1-3 weeks for uncommon parts
- **Service Speed**: 2-5 days typical
- **Call Center**: 444 6 543
- **Notes**: Reduced physical presence in Turkey compared to Samsung. OLED TV service limited to larger cities. Brand Service Plus extended warranty available for purchase.

## Bosch / Siemens / Profilo
- **Parent**: BSH Türkiye (Bosch und Siemens Hausgeräte)
- **Service Network**: 500+ yetkili servis noktası
- **Coverage**: Very good nationwide. Profilo network adds coverage in smaller cities.
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard. Bosch Home Connect appliances have digital warranty tracking.
- **Spare Parts**: Local warehouse in Turkey, good availability. Premium parts pricing.
- **Service Speed**: 1-3 days in cities
- **Call Center**: 444 6 333 (Bosch), 444 6 888 (Siemens), 444 7 888 (Profilo)
- **Notes**: Profilo is BSH's Turkey-specific budget brand. All three brands share service infrastructure. Bosch positioned premium, Siemens mid-premium, Profilo value. Local production for many white goods categories.

## Vestel / Regal
- **Parent**: Zorlu Holding
- **Service Network**: 500+ yetkili servis noktası
- **Coverage**: Very good nationwide, strong in smaller cities and rural areas
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard
- **Spare Parts**: Locally manufactured, very fast availability
- **Service Speed**: 1-3 days
- **Call Center**: 444 4 123
- **Notes**: Budget-friendly domestic brand. Regal is Vestel's sub-brand. Large-scale TV manufacturing in Manisa. Good value for money but build quality sometimes lower than Arçelik/Beko. Strong government/institutional sales.

## Dyson
- **Service Network**: Very limited -- 3 Dyson Demo Stores (Istanbul Zorlu, Istanbul Akasya, Ankara Kentpark) + handful of authorized service points
- **Coverage**: Istanbul and Ankara only for direct service. Other cities via cargo service.
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard
- **Spare Parts**: Imported from UK/Singapore. 1-4 weeks lead time.
- **Service Speed**: 3-10 business days (including shipping for non-Istanbul customers)
- **Call Center**: 0850 250 0539
- **Notes**: Premium pricing. Service for cities outside Istanbul/Ankara requires shipping product to service center. Consider this when buying -- local alternatives (Arzum, Philips) have much better service access. Battery replacements are costly.

## Xiaomi
- **Service Network**: 50+ Mi Store / authorized service points, rapidly expanding
- **Coverage**: Major cities well covered, smaller cities via Mi Store partners
- **Warranty**: 2 years for phones, varies by product category
- **Spare Parts**: Improving availability. Phone screens and batteries usually in stock at Mi Stores.
- **Service Speed**: 1-5 days for phones (at Mi Store), 5-10 days for ecosystem products
- **Call Center**: 0850 455 0 900
- **Notes**: Fast-growing service network. Robot vacuum and air purifier service still limited to major cities. Phone service good via Mi Stores. Ecosystem products (scooters, cameras) may need cargo service. Some products sold in Turkey lack local warranty -- buy from official channels (Mi Store, Trendyol official, Hepsiburada official).

## Apple
- **Service Network**: 4 Apple Store (Istanbul: Bağdat Caddesi, Zorlu Center, Akasya, Piazza) + 80+ Apple Yetkili Servis Sağlayıcısı (AYSS)
- **Coverage**: Apple Stores only in Istanbul. AYSS nationwide in major cities.
- **Warranty**: 1 year standard (AppleCare+ extends to 2-3 years)
- **Spare Parts**: Apple-controlled supply chain. Genuine parts at Apple Store/AYSS only.
- **Service Speed**: Apple Store walk-in: same-day for common repairs. AYSS: 2-7 days. Complex repairs: up to 14 days (shipped to regional repair center).
- **Call Center**: 0800 211 40 18
- **Notes**: AppleCare+ strongly recommended in Turkey due to high repair costs and TRY exchange rate impact. Screen repair costs can be 30-50% of new device price without AppleCare+. iPhone IMEI registration (2,247 TL+ in 2024) required for devices purchased abroad. Buy from authorized resellers for local warranty.

## Arzum
- **Service Network**: 400+ yetkili servis noktası
- **Coverage**: Excellent nationwide including small towns
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard
- **Spare Parts**: Locally manufactured, widely available. Replacement parts also sold directly to consumers via arzum.com.tr.
- **Service Speed**: 1-3 days
- **Call Center**: 444 0 200
- **Notes**: Turkey's leading small appliance brand. Çay makinesi, blender, tost makinesi, saç kurutma makinesi. Very strong after-sales for a small appliance brand. Okka (Turkish coffee machine) series has dedicated support. Consumer-replaceable parts (filters, blades) available online.

## Philips
- **Service Network**: Reduced -- approximately 100 service points remaining
- **Coverage**: Major cities only. Small city coverage declining.
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard
- **Spare Parts**: Imported, availability inconsistent for older models
- **Service Speed**: 3-7 days in cities with service points, longer elsewhere
- **Call Center**: 444 0 800
- **Notes**: Philips has significantly reduced operations in Turkey since 2023. Personal care and small kitchen appliances still sold but service infrastructure shrinking. For new purchases, consider Arzum or Bosch as alternatives with better local service. TV business sold to TP Vision (still branded Philips). Medical/lighting divisions separate from consumer products.

## Miele
- **Service Network**: 15-20 authorized service points
- **Coverage**: Istanbul, Ankara, İzmir, Antalya, Bursa primarily
- **Warranty**: 2 years standard (extendable to 5 years with Miele Care)
- **Spare Parts**: Imported from Germany. 1-3 weeks typical.
- **Service Speed**: 3-7 days in covered cities
- **Call Center**: 444 0 120
- **Notes**: Ultra-premium positioning. Service quality high but geographic coverage limited. Miele Center Istanbul for showroom + service. Products designed to last 20 years -- factor service accessibility into purchase decision for non-Istanbul buyers.
