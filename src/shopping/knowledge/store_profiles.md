# Store Profiles

## Trendyol
- **Type**: Marketplace + direct retail (Trendyol Express for grocery/fast delivery)
- **URL**: trendyol.com
- **Description**: Turkey's largest e-commerce platform. Originally fashion-focused, now covers all categories. Alibaba Group invested.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: Primarily marketplace with millions of third-party sellers. "Trendyol" branded items are direct.
- **Shipping**: Free shipping thresholds vary by seller (typically 200-300 TL). Trendyol Express: same-day/next-day for grocery/essentials. Standard: 2-5 business days.
- **Returns**: 15 days for most categories. Free return via Trendyol drop-off points or cargo pickup. Electronics: 15 days unopened, defect-only after opening.
- **Installments**: Up to 12 ay taksit depending on bank partnership. Frequent faizsiz campaigns.
- **Gotchas**: Marketplace seller quality varies wildly. Check seller rating (4.5+ preferred). "Trendyol Mağaza Puanı" matters. Some sellers ship from abroad (longer delivery). Price manipulation before sales -- always check Akakçe history.
- **Physical**: Trendyol Express dark stores in major cities. No retail showrooms. Dolap (second-hand) integration.

## Hepsiburada
- **Type**: Marketplace + direct retail ("Hepsiburada" storefront)
- **URL**: hepsiburada.com
- **Description**: Second largest platform. Stronger in electronics/appliances than Trendyol. Publicly traded (NASDAQ: HEPS).
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: Hybrid. "Hepsiburada" as seller = direct with better warranty handling. Third-party sellers clearly labeled.
- **Shipping**: Hepsiburada Premium membership for free shipping. Otherwise 50-200 TL threshold. HepsiJet own logistics network. 1-3 days typical.
- **Returns**: 15 days standard. Appliances: installation-day inspection critical -- reject visibly damaged items immediately. HepsiJet pickup for returns.
- **Installments**: Strong bank partnerships. Up to 12 ay. "Hepsiburada Premium + X Banka" combos for extra taksit.
- **Gotchas**: "Süper Fiyat" items often have limited stock, used as traffic drivers. Check if seller is "Hepsiburada" vs marketplace for warranty handling. Assembly/installation services available but book early.
- **Physical**: No retail stores. HepsiJet service points for pickup/return.

## Amazon TR (amazon.com.tr)
- **Type**: Marketplace + direct retail ("Amazon.com.tr" as seller)
- **URL**: amazon.com.tr
- **Description**: Smaller than Trendyol/Hepsiburada in Turkey but growing. Strong in books, electronics, imported goods.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: "Amazon.com.tr" = direct, better returns. Third-party with "Fulfillment by Amazon" also reliable.
- **Shipping**: Prime membership for free/fast shipping. Non-Prime: free above threshold (~200 TL). Prime same-day in Istanbul for eligible items.
- **Returns**: 30 days (best in market). Amazon-direct returns are seamless. Third-party seller returns can be slower.
- **Installments**: Limited compared to Trendyol/Hepsiburada. Fewer bank partnerships. Improving but still weaker.
- **Gotchas**: Product range smaller than competitors. Some listings are amazon.com (international) -- watch for import fees. Prime membership needed for best experience. Customer service quality higher than local competitors.
- **Physical**: None. Amazon Locker rollout limited.

## N11
- **Type**: Pure marketplace
- **URL**: n11.com
- **Description**: Marketplace platform. Good for finding niche/small sellers with competitive prices.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: 100% marketplace, no direct retail. All orders fulfilled by independent sellers.
- **Shipping**: Seller-dependent. Free shipping campaigns frequent. 2-5 days typical.
- **Returns**: 15 days. Return process depends on seller cooperation. Platform mediates disputes.
- **Installments**: Up to 12 ay with various banks. N11 often runs extra taksit campaigns.
- **Gotchas**: Seller quality inconsistent -- check reviews carefully. Dispute resolution slower than Trendyol/Hepsiburada. Can find good deals from small shops. Less buyer protection than larger platforms.
- **Physical**: None.

## MediaMarkt (TR)
- **Type**: Direct retail (own inventory)
- **URL**: mediamarkt.com.tr
- **Description**: German electronics retailer. Focused on electronics, appliances, gaming. Strong in-store experience.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: 100% direct retail with own inventory. What you see is what MediaMarkt sells.
- **Shipping**: Free above threshold. Store pickup (click & collect) available same-day. Standard 2-4 days.
- **Returns**: 15 days. In-store returns possible (faster than cargo). Open-box electronics accepted with original packaging.
- **Installments**: Excellent bank partnerships. Up to 12 ay faizsiz common on big-ticket items. Store-specific campaigns.
- **Gotchas**: Prices often slightly higher than marketplace sellers, but includes proper warranty/service. "Fiyat Eşleştirme" (price match) sometimes available in-store -- ask. Extended warranty (Ek Garanti) aggressively sold -- evaluate need case by case.
- **Physical**: 90+ stores across Turkey. Strong in Istanbul, Ankara, İzmir. In-store demo units for testing.

## Vatanbilgisayar
- **Type**: Direct retail (own inventory)
- **URL**: vatanbilgisayar.com
- **Description**: Turkish electronics chain. Strong in computers, components, peripherals. Tech-savvy customer base.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: 100% direct. Own supply chain.
- **Shipping**: Free above threshold. Store pickup available. 1-3 days in major cities.
- **Returns**: 14 days. Store returns accepted. Opened software/games excluded.
- **Installments**: Up to 12 ay. Frequent faizsiz campaigns, especially on laptops/PCs. "Vatan Kart" loyalty program.
- **Gotchas**: Best for PC components and peripherals -- competitive pricing. Website can be slow during campaigns. Stock accuracy online not always perfect -- call store to verify for rare items. Good technical advice in-store.
- **Physical**: 80+ stores. Staff generally knowledgeable about tech specs. In-store PC building service available.

## Koçtaş
- **Type**: Direct retail (own inventory + marketplace for extended range)
- **URL**: koctas.com.tr
- **Description**: Turkey's largest home improvement / DIY chain. Kingfisher Group partnership. Hardware, garden, bathroom, kitchen, paint, tools.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: Mostly direct inventory. Extended range from partner sellers on website.
- **Shipping**: Large item delivery available (appliances, furniture). Standard items 2-5 days. Free above threshold.
- **Returns**: 15 days. In-store returns for most items. Opened paint/custom-cut items excluded.
- **Installments**: Up to 9 ay typical. Campaign-dependent for faizsiz.
- **Gotchas**: Best prices on own-brand (Koçtaş, Dexter, GoodHome). Installation services bookable (plumbing, electrical, assembly). Weekend stores very crowded. Online stock may differ from store stock -- use "Mağazada Var" filter.
- **Physical**: 40+ large-format stores with garden centers. Strong in Istanbul, expanding Anatolia.

## IKEA TR
- **Type**: Direct retail (own inventory)
- **URL**: ikea.com.tr
- **Description**: Swedish furniture giant. Furniture, home decoration, kitchen, storage, textiles.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: 100% IKEA products. No third-party sellers.
- **Shipping**: Delivery fees based on order size/distance. Small parcel: 80-150 TL. Large furniture: 200-500 TL. Click & collect: free.
- **Returns**: 365 days unopened, 180 days opened (best return policy in Turkey). Must have receipt/order confirmation.
- **Installments**: Up to 12 ay with partner banks. IKEA FAMILY card benefits for extra deals.
- **Gotchas**: Assembly required for most furniture (montaj hizmeti available for fee ~300-800 TL). Stock availability issues on popular items -- check online before visiting. TRY price adjustments happen 2-3x per year following global IKEA pricing. Kitchen planning service free but book weeks ahead. Delivery slots fill up on weekends.
- **Physical**: 10 stores (Istanbul Bayrampaşa, Istanbul Ümraniye, Ankara, İzmir, Antalya, Bursa, etc.). Order & Collection Points in smaller cities.

## Migros
- **Type**: Direct retail (supermarket chain + online grocery)
- **URL**: migros.com.tr / Migros Sanal Market app
- **Description**: Turkey's leading premium supermarket. Wide grocery range, fresh produce, household goods. Online delivery strong.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: Direct retail. "Migros Hemen" for fast delivery from dark stores.
- **Shipping**: Migros Sanal Market: scheduled delivery (free above 400-500 TL). Migros Hemen: 30-60 min delivery, higher minimums. Trendyol/Getir integration for express.
- **Returns**: Perishables replaced/refunded on delivery issues. Packaged goods: standard consumer rights apply.
- **Installments**: Not applicable for grocery. Money Kart loyalty program for points/discounts.
- **Gotchas**: "Money" loyalty card essential for advertised prices. "5M Migros" largest stores with best range. Online prices may differ from store prices. "Migros Sanal Market" vs "Migros Hemen" are different services with different pricing. Virtual market (Sanal Market) has wider range but slower delivery.
- **Physical**: 2,500+ stores across Turkey (Migros, 5M Migros, Migros Jet, Macrocenter).

## Getir
- **Type**: Quick commerce / dark store delivery
- **URL**: getir.com / Getir app
- **Description**: Ultra-fast grocery/essentials delivery (10-30 min). Pioneer of quick commerce model from Turkey.
- **Marketplace vs Direct**: Direct retail from own dark stores. Curated product range.
- **Shipping**: Free above minimum order (~150-200 TL). Delivery fee 15-30 TL under minimum. 10-30 minute delivery promise.
- **Returns**: Refund/replacement for quality issues via app. Simple process.
- **Installments**: Not applicable (low basket sizes). Credit/debit card payment.
- **Gotchas**: Prices 10-20% higher than supermarket for convenience premium. Product range limited to ~2,000-3,000 SKUs per dark store. GetirYemek (food delivery) and GetirÇarşı (marketplace) are separate services. Best for urgent/forgotten items, not weekly grocery hauls. Coverage limited to urban areas.
- **Physical**: Dark stores only (not customer-accessible). Operates in 10+ Turkish cities.
