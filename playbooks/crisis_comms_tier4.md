# Crisis Comms Playbook — Tier 4: Existential / Legal

**Trigger:** Lawsuit filed, regulatory enforcement action, material public accusation by
a major figure (journalist/influencer/regulator), or threat that could end the product.

**This playbook is authored by the founder, not drafted by the agent.**
Agent role: freeze marketing, surface context dump, remind on time-sensitive tasks, log all actions.

---

## CRITICAL: First 30 minutes

1. **DO NOT POST ANYTHING.** All public comms must go through counsel.
2. **Engage counsel immediately.** Founder_action "counsel engaged?" with two-button ack.
   This is not optional at Tier 4.
3. **Freeze ALL marketing, announcements, and outreach.** Auto-triggered at Tier 4 open.
   A2/B1/A7 are all paused for this product.
4. **Document the trigger event.** Exact words, URL, timestamp, who said it, to whom.
5. **Do not delete or alter anything.** Litigation hold applies immediately.

## Context dump (auto-surfaced by agent on Tier 4 open)

The agent surfaces a founder_action with full context:
- Active missions for this product.
- Recent external comms (B9 audit log — last 30 days).
- Recent crisis_events and incident history.
- Pending founder_actions that might be relevant.
- Key contacts in relationships (A10) with legal/press/investor tags.

## Counsel-led response tracks

### Regulatory action
- Respond via counsel only. No public statement without legal clearance.
- Preserve all records (DB backup, email archive, code history).
- Prepare compliance evidence (Z2 migration_apply logs, Z6 vendor audit trail).

### Lawsuit
- Service of process → immediate counsel engagement.
- All internal comms potentially discoverable — assume everything is on record.
- DO NOT discuss lawsuit details over Telegram/email without counsel guidance.

### Major public accusation
- Prepare factual record (not a rebuttal post — that escalates).
- Counsel decides: silence, factual correction, or statement.
- Investor notification may be required (check term sheet covenants).

### Existential reputation threat
- Brief key stakeholders (investors, advisors, major customers) before public spread.
- One spokesperson only (founder); no other team member speaks publicly.
- Consider: product pause vs. product shutdown vs. rebrand — counsel advises.

## Marketing freeze scope (Tier 4)

All A2 launch activities, B1 email sequences, B2 changelog publishes, and A7 outreach
are suspended for the affected product until founder explicitly runs `/crisis resume <product>`.

Other products continue running — freeze is product-scoped.

## Emergency founder_action (auto-emitted at Tier 4 open)

Urgency: P0_BLOCKING. Surfaced immediately. Contains:
- Crisis summary.
- Recommended first calls (counsel, relevant investors if warranted).
- Time-sensitive deadlines from playbook.
- Link to this playbook.

## Resumption criteria

Before running `/crisis resume`:
1. Counsel has confirmed it is safe to resume external comms.
2. Any regulatory / litigation filing deadlines are met or tracked.
3. Postmortem / corrective-action plan is documented.
4. Investor/advisor notifications have been made if required.
