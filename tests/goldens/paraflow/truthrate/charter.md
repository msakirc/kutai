# Product Charter — TruthRate

## 1) Product Positioning

TruthRate is a universal review platform that serves everyday consumers and business owners seeking transparent, credible information about anything in the world. Unlike traditional review platforms limited to specific categories, we enable users to review and share verified facts about any product, service, or business—from a specific cheese brand to a local barbershop. We separate subjective opinions from objective facts, creating a dual-layer credibility system where truth is protected and community reputation matters.

## 2) Brand Keywords

- **Universal** — Users can search for and review anything without category limitations, making honest information accessible everywhere
- **Transparent** — Clear separation between personal opinions and factual claims ensures users understand what's subjective versus objective
- **Credible** — Reputation systems for reviewers and fact-checking mechanisms build trust in the information shared
- **Privacy-First** — Username-based authentication and strong data protection respect user privacy while encouraging honest contributions
- **Fair** — Business owners can claim their listings and respond to any review or fact, ensuring balanced dialogue

## 3) Core Problem / JTBD

When people need to make purchasing decisions or learn about businesses, products, or services, they struggle to find trustworthy information that distinguishes between personal opinions and verifiable facts. Existing review platforms are limited to specific categories, mix subjective reviews with objective claims, and lack mechanisms to verify important information like ownership conflicts or health violations. Users want to know both "what do people think?" and "what is factually true?" but current platforms blur these lines, making it difficult to trust the information they find.

## 4) Goals & Mission

- **Mission:** To create a universal platform where truth and transparency empower better decisions, by separating facts from opinions and building community credibility through reputation.

- **Desired Outcomes (descriptive):**
  - Users feel confident making decisions based on clearly separated reviews and facts
  - Business owners actively engage with feedback through subscription-based claiming
  - Contributors build reputation by providing helpful reviews and accurate facts
  - The platform maintains information quality through community fact-checking and admin oversight
  - Privacy remains protected while encouraging authentic participation
  - Engagement remains high through low-friction contribution methods like star ratings

## 5) Solutions We Own

### Universal Search & Discovery
- **What it solves:** Enables users to find and review literally anything without platform category restrictions
- **Typical path:** Users search for any business, product, or service by name; the system returns existing reviews and facts or allows creation of new entries; users can browse by categories or discover through community contributions
- **Outcome for the user:** Users find credible information about anything they're interested in, whether popular or obscure
- **Guiding principles:** Flexibility / Comprehensive coverage

### Dual-Layer Information System
- **What it solves:** Separates subjective opinions from objective facts, allowing users to understand different types of information
- **Typical path:** Users submit reviews expressing personal experiences or contribute facts with supporting evidence; the system clearly distinguishes these two types; other users can rate helpfulness of reviews and fact-check claims
- **Outcome for the user:** Users can quickly assess both community sentiment and verifiable information, making informed decisions
- **Boundaries:** Facts require user guarantee of truth and encourage supporting media/links; system provides UI prompts but doesn't enforce strict verification at submission
- **Guiding principles:** Clarity / Truth-seeking / Transparency

### Credibility & Reputation System
- **What it solves:** Builds trust in user contributions through helpfulness metrics for reviews, fact-checking for claims, and overall user reputation scores
- **Typical path:** Users contribute reviews and facts; community members mark reviews as helpful or fact-check claims; the system calculates individual contribution quality and overall user reputation; high-reputation users gain visibility
- **Outcome for the user:** Users can trust information from high-reputation contributors and feel motivated to provide quality content
- **Guiding principles:** Meritocratic / Community-driven / Quality-focused

### Business Claiming & Response
- **What it solves:** Gives business owners a voice to respond to reviews and facts while generating revenue through subscription packages
- **Typical path:** Business owners claim their listings through tiered subscription plans; they gain access to a business dashboard (web-optimized) where they can respond to reviews, address factual claims, upload supporting media, and manage their presence
- **Outcome for the user:** Business owners participate in dialogue, defend their reputation, and provide their perspective on claims
- **Boundaries:** Only verified business owners can claim; responses are clearly marked as from business owners; subscription required for claiming
- **Guiding principles:** Fairness / Revenue-generating / Accountability

### Engagement & Contribution
- **What it solves:** Encourages broad participation through multiple contribution methods ranging from quick star ratings to detailed reviews and fact reporting
- **Typical path:** Users choose their level of engagement—quick star rating for casual feedback, written reviews for detailed opinions, or fact reporting with evidence for important information; mobile-first design makes contributions effortless
- **Outcome for the user:** Users contribute at their comfort level, building personal reputation while helping the community
- **Guiding principles:** Low-friction / Mobile-optimized / Rewarding

### Content Moderation & Admin Control
- **What it solves:** Maintains platform quality and handles edge cases through manual admin intervention
- **Typical path:** Admin monitors flagged content, reviews reported facts and reviews, and can manually remove or moderate content; system provides admin panel for oversight
- **Outcome for the user:** Platform maintains credibility and removes harmful or false content when necessary
- **Boundaries:** Admin intervention is manual and case-by-case, not automated filtering
- **Guiding principles:** Quality control / Trust & Safety
