### Ramadan-Specific Notes
- Grocery platforms run daily iftar specials
- Gıda (food) campaigns intensify the last 2 weeks
- Bayram öncesi: clothing/shoes peak buying period
- Gold/jewelry traditionally purchased for Bayram gifts

|--------|--------------|-------------|-------|

| BİM | Friday | Thursday evening | Aktüel products sell out fast, arrive early |

| Migros | Variable (app-based) | Continuous | Personalized Money points offers |

### Aktüel Product Strategy
- Non-food specials (electronics, kitchen, textiles) arrive once, limited quantity
- No restocking -- sold out means gone
- Best strategy: check catalog night before, arrive at opening (08:00-08:30)
- A101/BİM/ŞOK aktüel products are often surprisingly good quality for price

## Currency & Import Considerations

### USD/TRY Impact
- **Imported electronics** (phones, laptops, GPUs): prices track USD/TRY closely, updated weekly
- **Domestic appliances** (Arçelik, Beko, Vestel): less volatile, adjusted monthly/quarterly
- **Global brands assembled in TR** (BSH/Bosch-Siemens Türkiye): moderate sensitivity
- **Food/FMCG**: domestic production shields from FX, but imported ingredients add pressure
- **Price memory**: stores often delay price drops when TRY strengthens -- compare historical prices

### Customs & Import Rules
- International orders above **€150** (or equivalent) subject to customs duty
- Customs duty rate: typically 18-20% (product-dependent) + 20% KDV on total
- Phone/tablet IMEI registration required within 120 days (costs ~15,000 TL+ registration fee)
- One phone IMEI registration per passport per 3 years (for travelers)
- Electronics from Amazon.com/Amazon.de: factor in customs + KDV
- Amazon TR, Trendyol, Hepsiburada: domestic sellers, no customs issues
- AliExpress: orders under €150 arrive via PTT, over €150 held at customs

## Installment Culture (Taksit)

### How Taksit Works
- Near-universal on electronics/appliance purchases above ~500 TL
- Common tiers: **2, 3, 6, 9, 12 ay taksit**
- Interest-free (faizsiz) taksit: negotiated between store and bank
- Specific card required: e.g., "Garanti BBVA ile 9 ay faizsiz"
- World/Platinum/Business cards often get extra installments

### Taksit Strategy
- Interest-free installments = free money, always prefer over peşin (cash) if available
- Check: store page shows "X banka ile Y taksit" badges
- Some categories excluded: grocery, fuel, gold typically no taksit
- Credit card limit must cover full amount even for taksit
- "Taksit + Kart Puanı" combos: pay with points + spread remainder

## Market Structure Notes

### Domestic Brands & Service
- **Arçelik/Beko**: Koç Group, largest domestic. 4,500+ authorized service points. Strong warranty support
- **Vestel**: Zorlu Group, budget-friendly domestic. Good service network in smaller cities
- **Arzum**: Small appliances (çay makinesi, blender). Local favorite, good parts availability
- **Altus**: Arçelik's budget sub-brand. Same service network as Beko

### International Brand Presence
- **Samsung**: strong TR presence, local assembly, good service
- **LG**: reduced physical presence, service via authorized dealers
- **Philips**: significantly reduced TR operations since 2023, limited service
- **Dyson**: premium positioning, limited authorized service points (Istanbul/Ankara mainly)
- **Xiaomi**: aggressive pricing, rapidly expanding authorized service network
- **Apple**: no official stores outside Istanbul (4 stores), authorized resellers nationwide

### Price Comparison Habits
- Turkish consumers heavily use **Akakçe** (akakce.com) for price comparison
- **Cimri** (cimri.com) as secondary price tracker
- Historical price tracking available on both -- check before buying
- "En ucuz" (cheapest) culture strong, but service/warranty increasingly valued
