# Business Owner "The Engaged Proprietor"

Carlos Rodriguez

Owner and Manager, Local Restaurant Chain

**Age:** 42

**Location:** Miami, Florida

> "I need to protect my business reputation and respond to both fair criticism and false claims before they hurt my livelihood."

## About Carlos

Carlos owns three restaurant locations and takes customer feedback seriously. He actively monitors review platforms but struggles with limited response options and the inability to address false claims that could damage his business. He's willing to invest in tools that help him manage his online reputation, especially if they give him a direct channel to respond to concerns and showcase his commitment to quality.

## Behavioral Considerations

- Primarily accesses review platforms from his desktop computer during administrative work hours
- Responds quickly to negative reviews or factual claims that could harm his business
- Values being able to upload photos and documentation to support his responses
- Willing to pay for subscription services that provide meaningful business value
- Monitors competitor reviews to understand market positioning
- Prefers professional, feature-rich interfaces over simplified mobile experiences

## Frustrations

- Can't respond to reviews on most platforms unless he goes through a complicated claiming process
- Factual claims and subjective opinions are mixed together, making it hard to address each appropriately
- Limited ability to provide context or evidence when customers make inaccurate claims
- Free platforms don't offer enough features to manage his reputation effectively
- Mobile apps don't provide the full functionality he needs for business management

## Goals

- Claim and manage all his business listings from a single professional dashboard
- Respond promptly and professionally to both reviews and factual claims
- Provide evidence and context when addressing inaccurate information
- Demonstrate commitment to customer satisfaction through active engagement
- Understand which subscription tier offers the best value for his business needs
- Monitor all locations efficiently from one interface

## Tasks

- Claim business listings through subscription purchase
- Monitor new reviews and factual claims across all locations
- Respond to customer reviews with personalized messages
- Address factual claims by providing counter-evidence or clarification
- Upload photos and documentation to support responses
- Manage subscription and billing preferences
- Track engagement metrics to measure response effectiveness
- Browse competitor profiles to understand market positioning
# Reviewer "The Truth Seeker"

Maya Chen

Marketing Coordinator, remote work environment

**Age:** 29

**Location:** Austin, Texas

> "I want to make smart choices based on real facts, not just marketing hype or fake reviews."

## About Maya

Maya is a conscious consumer who researches thoroughly before making any purchase decision. She regularly reads reviews across multiple platforms but has grown frustrated with the lack of trustworthy information and category limitations on existing review sites. She values transparency and wants to contribute her own experiences to help others, but only when she feels her input will actually make a difference.

## Behavioral Considerations

- Checks reviews on her phone throughout the day, often while standing in stores or browsing online
- Trusts reviewers with established credibility more than anonymous one-time contributors
- Willing to spend time writing detailed reviews when she has strong opinions or important information to share
- Quickly dismisses platforms that feel cluttered or make it hard to distinguish facts from opinions
- Appreciates when she can quickly leave a star rating without writing a full review

## Frustrations

- Existing review platforms are limited to specific categories, so she can't review everything she encounters
- Difficult to tell which reviews are genuine and which are biased or paid for
- Important factual information gets buried in subjective opinion reviews
- No way to track if her contributions are actually helping other people
- Privacy concerns about sharing personal email addresses for account registration

## Goals

- Find reliable, truthful information about any product or service before making decisions
- Contribute meaningful reviews and facts that help others avoid bad experiences
- Build a reputation as a trusted contributor whose opinions matter
- Discover important facts about businesses that aren't widely known
- Protect her privacy while participating in online communities

## Tasks

- Search for and read reviews about specific products, services, or businesses
- Submit quick star ratings for things she's experienced
- Write detailed reviews when she has important experiences to share
- Report factual information with supporting evidence when she discovers something important
- Fact-check claims made by other users to maintain information quality
- Track her own reputation score and contribution metrics
- Browse helpful reviews from other high-reputation users
